Paper Reproductions
=========================

:code:`tms-kit` supports reproducing the following papers:
    * `Toy Models of Superposition <https://transformer-circuits.pub/2022/toy_model/index.html>`_. Reproduction is available as `experiments/bottleneck_tms <https://github.com/dtch1997/tms-kit/blob/main/experiments/bottleneck_tms/run.py>`_. 
    * `Toy Models of Feature Absorption <https://www.lesswrong.com/posts/kcg58WhRxFA9hv9vN/toy-models-of-feature-absorption-in-saes>`_. Reproduction is available as `experiments/feature_absorption <https://github.com/dtch1997/tms-kit/blob/main/experiments/feature_absorption/run.py>`_.
